1) Iteration constructs:
               a) while
               b) do while
               c) for

2) The difference between a "while" and a "do while" loop is the place of condition. In the first case the condition
will be in the beginning of the loop. In the second case the condition will be in the end of the loop. 

3)By onditional expression is meant the choice between 2 sub-expressions. If the condition is true the choice will be the operand 
between "?" and ":". If it is false choice will be the operand after ":".
